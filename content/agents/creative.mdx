# isA Creative

Your AI content creation assistant.

## Overview

isA Creative helps you generate, edit, and refine creative content. From blog posts to marketing copy, it understands context and produces high-quality output.

## Features

### Content Generation
- Blog posts and articles
- Marketing copy
- Social media content
- Email templates

### Editing & Refinement
- Grammar and style checking
- Tone adjustment
- SEO optimization
- Readability improvement

### Research
- Topic research
- Fact-checking
- Source finding
- Competitive analysis

### Multi-Format Support
- Long-form articles
- Short-form content
- Headlines and titles
- Summaries and abstracts

## Quick Start

```bash
# Install isA Creative CLI
pip install isa-creative

# Generate content
isa-creative "Write a blog post about AI trends"
```

## Skills

isA Creative comes with specialized skills:
- `copywriter` - Marketing and ad copy
- `blogger` - Long-form content
- `social-media` - Platform-specific content
- `editor` - Editing and refinement
- `researcher` - Research and fact-checking

## Configuration

```yaml
# .isa-creative.yaml
skills:
  - copywriter
  - blogger
tone: professional
target_audience: developers
```
